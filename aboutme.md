Hello, my name is **Jesús Muñoz a.k,a. "*omiK*"** and I'm bringing to you **Perverso d20!** 

I'm creating bilingual (spanish and english) short stories, dungeons and OSR / system-agnostic adventures for tabletop roleplaying games.

I will post one adventure fortnightly(ish) and some short stories and dungeons now an then.

I believe on **Free Cultural Works** so all the content will be released under Creative Commons [CC BY-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/deed) license.

If you enjoy this content, firstly, thanks a lot and **please consider supporting Perverso d20**  buying us a coffee or becoming a backer!
